About Slipknot

Slipknot is an American heavy metal band formed in 1995 in Des Moines, Iowa.

Discography

Slipknot (1999)

Slipknot's self-titled debut album, released in 1999, showcased their aggressive and unique sound.

Iowa (2001)

The band's second album, Iowa, was released in 2001 and further solidified their place in the metal scene.

Vol. 3: (The Subliminal Verses) (2004)

Vol. 3: (The Subliminal Verses) showcased a more melodic side of Slipknot's music while still maintaining their heavy sound.

All Hope Is Gone (2008)

All Hope Is Gone was released in 2008, featuring a mix of aggressive tracks and more experimental elements.
